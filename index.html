Sotto Voce is a non profit making company based in Paphos, Cyprus, and it is the initiative of pianist and musicologist Dr. Galina Dimova-Georgieva and video artist Yiannos Economou. Also collaborators is composer Dimitris Tsoukas and printmaker Evgenia Vasiloudi. It provides support for the the activities of artistic group In Sutu.
