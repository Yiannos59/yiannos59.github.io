Sotto Voce is a nonprofit making company based in Paphos, Cyprus, and it is the initiative of pianist and musicologist Dr. Galina Dimova-Georgieva and video artist Yiannos Economou. Also collaborators are composer Dimitris Tsoukas and printmaker Evgenia Vasiloudi. It provides support for the activities of artistic group In Sutu.
