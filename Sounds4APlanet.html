Sounds for a Wounded Planet

SOUNDS FOR A WOUNDED PLANET, SERRES


Production: In Sutu

A parallel event to the exhibition Art For A Wounded Planet in Serres. Galina Dimova-Georgieva performed piano works by international composers or pieces specifically written for her collaboration with video artist Yiannos Economou, who was responsible for the video and photography projections. It took place on the 4th October 2025, at the Friends of Literature and Arts Society, Serres.
